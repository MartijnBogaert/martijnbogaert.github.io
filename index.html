Hi, I'm Martijn!

Thanks for visiting my website! Let me first introduce myself. I'm a 22 year old software developer from Bruges, Belgium. I'm mostly focused on web and mobile development and got my bachelor's degree in Applied Information Technology at Ghent University College, HOGENT for short. Working full-stack is what I love most. Concerning backend, I'm best trained in working with .NET Core, yet I'm fine with other technologies as well. The same goes for frontend, although when talking mobile, I prefer iOS.

It goes without saying I'm a real computer fanatic. What's more interesting, though, is my many years of looking up to anything Apple-related. I guess you could call me a real fanboy. Computes aside, I also love submering myself in space, as well as music - I play the piano and the saxophone. But most of all, I can't get enough of football, the most important side issue in life.
